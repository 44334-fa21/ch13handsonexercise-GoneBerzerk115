Learning English Basics

The Many Theres

In the Enlish language there are 3 different types of theres.

"There" is used when describing the location of something. (Ex: The students went to the playground over there to have fun!)

"Their" is used when describing something that a person owns. (Ex: That ball was stolen from the teacher, it is theirs.)

"They're" is used when describing what someone or something is/is doing. (Ex: The kids got hurt when they fell but they're fine!)

The 2 Yours

In the Enlish language there are 2 different types of yours.

"Your" is used when describing something that somebody owns. (Ex: Your car is very cool!)

"You're" is used when describing someone or what they are doing. (Ex: You're very pretty!)

All the Tos

In the Enlish language there are 3 different types of tos.

"To" is used when describing where someone is going/what they are doing. (Ex: I'm going to the bank.)

"Two" is used when stating the number 2. (Ex: There are 2 people over there!)

"Too" is used when desccribing something like they also did something or like something is a lot of something. (Ex: You're too kind! or Ex: You threw the ball too!)

The Desert for Dessert

"Desert" is for when you are addressing a massive body of sand. (Ex: I almost died from the heat in the desert!)

"Dessert" is for what you eat after you main course in a meal. It is the sweets that everybody loves. One trick is you always want seconds of dessert (2 S's) (Ex: I would like the chocolate cake for dessert!)
